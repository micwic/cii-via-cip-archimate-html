Master Person & User Registry Administration - Business Process, Application Usage, Technology Support ()
This model serves as an illustrative example, demonstrating the benefits of a meta-model-based repository. It encapsulates business processes and objects for the administration of persons and users, while integrating the associated applications and technologies that are actually used in the CII via CIP proof of concept. Developed within the context of the proof of concept, the model is intentionally generic, showcasing a structured, multi-layered approach that spans the business, application, and technology domains.
