Dutch Smart Meter Requirements P1 Companion Standard ()
DSMR-P1 Companion standard for an Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) system for electricity, thermal (heat & cold), gas, water and hot water meters. The scope of this standard is the end-consumer (P1) interface for Residential electricity meters, Residential thermal (heat & cold) meters, Residential gas meters, Residential water meter.
