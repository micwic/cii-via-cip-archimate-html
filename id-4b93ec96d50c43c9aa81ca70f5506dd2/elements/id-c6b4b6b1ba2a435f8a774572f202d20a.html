Customer Service Request Management ()
Set of functions and processes dedicated to handling service requests. **Note:** Regardless of the level of automation or the systems and applications implemented, the service request and its execution status must be communicated to the customer relations entity. This ensures that they can fulfill their information duties when necessary.
