**Abandon the Project**: A formal decision to discontinue the project following an evaluation of its outcomes, feasibility, or alignment with organizational objectives. This decision may result from factors such as unmet criteria, unviable solutions, changes in priorities, or resource constraints. It includes documenting the rationale for the decision, lessons learned, and any residual value from the project for future initiatives or reference.
