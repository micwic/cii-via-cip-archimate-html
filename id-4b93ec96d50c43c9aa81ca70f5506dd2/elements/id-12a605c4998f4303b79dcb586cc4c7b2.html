Analyze business architecture and develop solution architecture vision ()
The task "Analyze business architecture and develop solution architecture vision" involves assessing the organization's current and target business architecture to understand its capabilities, processes, and strategic objectives. This analysis aims to identify key business functions, roles, and information flows that support the desired outcomes. The insights gathered are then used to outline a solution architecture vision that aligns with business goals and provides a high-level roadmap for future development, ensuring coherence between business requirements and technical implementation. This task includes: 1. **Identifying Core Business Functions and Capabilities**: Establish the primary functions, processes, and capabilities within the business layer that are necessary to support strategic objectives. 2. **Assessing Current and Target Business Architecture**: Understand the existing structure and identify gaps or opportunities for improvement in alignment with business goals. 3. **Defining Solution Architecture Vision**: Create a high-level representation of the solution that aligns with the business requirements, supporting both current and future needs. 4. **Mapping Business Requirements to Architecture Layers**: Ensure the vision addresses the interdependencies and aligns the business layer requirements with the application and technology layers. This approach provides a foundational structure for the business architecture and a guiding vision for subsequent, more detailed phases in the development cycle.
