CII via CIP Customer Interactions <<BPA>> - UI ()
Interactions with customers who have submitted service requests are facilitated through screens generated from the execution of case models in Flowable. A comprehensive interaction model is made available through integration with the @flowable/work-views portal. The screens generated by Flowable are seamlessly embedded within a dedicated web section of the B2C portal, implemented via a specific DOM node configured with relevant properties. Interactions involving human stakeholders occur entirely within the portal, driven directly by the execution of case and process models designed in Flowable Design. This integration requires no further development efforts, ensuring efficient and streamlined deployment of human interactions as part of automated business processes.
