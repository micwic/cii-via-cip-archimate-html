CII via CIP involves different systems and potentially consumers interactions ()
The contractual situation is established from the ERP (The situation may have been established upstream in the B2C portal). Potentially necessary conversations with the consumer are conducted through the B2C portal, possibly via customer service (to be avoided for cost reasons). Technical feasibility and execution is established through the HES. The end-to-end activation and deactivation process must be orchestrated to achieve the desired result and ensure data consistency across the different systems. Inevitable exceptions must be managed by orchestration to avoid generating excessive costs for their resolution.
