### **CII via CIP Product Team** The **CII via CIP Product Team** focuses on delivering the Consumer Information Interface (CII) via the Consumer Information Port (CIP) based on previously defined architectures. The team is streamlined for efficiency, with the flexibility to draw on technical resources and expertise as needed to achieve its objectives. --- ### **Team Members and Responsibilities** 1. **CII via CIP Product Owner** - **Responsibility:** Owns the product vision and roadmap, translating high-level business and technical objectives into a prioritized product backlog. - Collaborates with the Solution Architect and Business Owner to ensure the backlog reflects stakeholder needs and pre-defined architectural principles. - Acts as the primary point of contact for stakeholders and the development team. - **Engages technical experts** for specific tasks when additional expertise is required. 2. **Solution Architect** - **Responsibility:** Provides the architectural framework and technical guidance necessary for the product backlog. - Ensures the solution aligns with the pre-defined architecture, regulatory standards, and integration requirements. - Supports the Product Owner by detailing technical dependencies and constraints. - **Collaborates with technical specialists** to address complex architectural challenges or emerging issues. 3. **Business Owner** - **Responsibility:** Ensures the product delivers business value and aligns with strategic objectives. - Validates the product backlog and ensures alignment with business priorities and regulatory requirements. - Provides high-level direction and resolves conflicts regarding business needs. - **Consults subject matter experts** when necessary to ensure the solution meets business and regulatory requirements. 4. **Project Management Team Members** - **Responsibility:** Facilitate the operational execution of the project, ensuring timelines, budgets, and resources are managed effectively. - Support the Product Owner in coordinating team activities and ensuring smooth communication between stakeholders and team members. - Monitor progress and identify risks, ensuring mitigation strategies are in place. - **Organize access to required expertise** and resources to maintain project momentum. --- ### **Key Team Focus** The primary responsibility of the **CII via CIP Product Team** is to create and manage the **product backlog** based on the predefined architectures. This involves: - Translating architectural blueprints into actionable items. - Prioritizing backlog items based on business value, technical dependencies, and compliance requirements. - Maintaining clear communication between the Product Owner, Solution Architect, and Business Owner to ensure alignment. - **Engaging technical resources and expertise** on-demand to address specific challenges or requirements. --- ### **Advantages of a Focused and Flexible Team** - **Efficiency:** A lean team structure minimizes overhead while enabling access to specialized expertise. - **Flexibility:** The ability to draw on additional resources ensures the team can adapt to technical or operational challenges. - **Alignment:** Close collaboration among core team members ensures the backlog is both comprehensive and actionable, while external expertise enhances execution.
