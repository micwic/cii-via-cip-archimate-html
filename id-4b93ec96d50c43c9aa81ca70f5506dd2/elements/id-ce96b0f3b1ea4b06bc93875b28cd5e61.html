CII via CIP Product Owner ()
### **CII via CIP Product Owner** The **CII via CIP Product Owner** is responsible for managing the product lifecycle of the Consumer Information Interface (CII) delivered through the Consumer Information Port (CIP) on smart meters. This role ensures the product meets legal, technical, and business requirements while delivering value to stakeholders and end-users. --- ### **Key Responsibilities** 1. **Product Vision and Roadmap** - Define and communicate the vision for the CII via CIP product, ensuring alignment with organizational and regulatory goals. - Develop and maintain a product roadmap that reflects business priorities, customer needs, and compliance requirements. 2. **Stakeholder Management** - Act as the primary liaison between business stakeholders, technical teams, and regulatory bodies. - Ensure stakeholder expectations are managed and their requirements are reflected in the product. 3. **Backlog Management** - Create, prioritize, and manage the product backlog, ensuring alignment with strategic goals. - Define user stories, acceptance criteria, and detailed requirements for development teams. 4. **Compliance and Standards Adherence** - Ensure the product complies with relevant standards and regulations (e.g., Swiss LApEl, IDIS, DSMR P1). - Collaborate with compliance officers to address legal and regulatory changes. 5. **Customer-Centric Focus** - Gather and analyze feedback from end-users (e.g., utility customers) to identify areas for improvement. - Ensure the product delivers a seamless and valuable experience for customers. 6. **Technical Oversight** - Work closely with Solution Architects and development teams to ensure technical feasibility and performance. - Oversee integration of the CII with backend systems, customer portals, and analytics platforms. 7. **Performance Monitoring and Optimization** - Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) for the CII via CIP solution. - Identify and implement improvements to enhance functionality, scalability, and reliability. 8. **Collaboration with Teams** - Coordinate with technical, operational, and security teams to deliver a secure and efficient solution. - Ensure smooth communication between cross-functional teams throughout the product lifecycle. 9. **Documentation and Communication** - Maintain up-to-date documentation of product features, compliance requirements, and development progress. - Report on product performance, risks, and roadmap updates to stakeholders. 10. **Innovation and Future Planning** - Identify opportunities for future enhancements and new features that align with business and regulatory needs. - Stay informed on emerging trends in smart metering and customer information systems. --- ### **Importance of the Role** The **CII via CIP Product Owner** ensures the successful delivery and continuous improvement of the product, balancing technical and regulatory requirements with business and customer needs. This role bridges strategy and execution, ensuring the product delivers value and compliance in a highly regulated environment.
