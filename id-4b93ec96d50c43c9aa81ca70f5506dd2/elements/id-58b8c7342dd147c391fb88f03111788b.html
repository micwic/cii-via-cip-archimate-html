### **Business Owner** The **Business Owner** is a key stakeholder in a project or organization, typically responsible for ensuring that the business outcomes of a project, product, or service align with the organization's goals and deliver value. #### **TOGAF Context** In TOGAF, the **Business Owner** represents the business perspective, focusing on defining and prioritizing business requirements, ensuring alignment with organizational strategies, and validating that the delivered solution meets business needs. This role works closely with architects and other stakeholders to ensure the architecture supports business capabilities. #### **HERMES Context** In the HERMES methodology, the **Business Owner** is encompassed by the role of the **Mandant** (Project Owner) or the **User Representatives**, depending on the scope. The **Mandant** provides strategic direction and ensures the project aligns with business objectives, while **User Representatives** bring detailed knowledge of business needs and processes, ensuring these are accurately reflected in the project deliverables. #### General Responsibilities - Define business goals and expected outcomes. - Validate alignment of the project or solution with business needs. - Prioritize requirements based on business impact. - Provide governance and decision-making from a business perspective. This interpretation ensures consistency across methodologies while adapting to their nuances.
