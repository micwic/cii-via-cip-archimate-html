**CII via CIP Solution Architecture Decision Report**: A document summarizing the key architectural decisions made during the CII via CIP project. It captures the rationale, alternatives considered, and justifications for selected approaches related to the design and implementation of the solution. The report also includes a formal decision on whether to proceed with the proposed solution or not, based on the outcomes of the Proof of Concept (PoC) phase, feasibility analysis, and alignment with project objectives. It serves as a reference for stakeholders, ensuring transparency and alignment on architectural choices, and provides a basis for future enhancements or iterations of the solution.
