**Initialization Phase**: Assess the project's feasibility, define objectives, identify stakeholders, and establish the project framework. This phase includes planning the required logistical and organizational setup to ensure the project is well-prepared for execution, with a clear structure, resources, and risk evaluation. *This definition applies to the preliminary phase of TOGAF, extended with the more detailed project management guidance provided by Hermès.*
