**Continue the Project**: A formal decision to proceed with the next phases of the project after a successful evaluation of initial outcomes, such as a Proof of Concept (PoC) or preliminary studies. This decision confirms that the project remains viable, aligns with organizational objectives, and has the necessary support and resources to advance. It marks a transition to detailed planning, implementation, or scaling, depending on the project's development stage.
