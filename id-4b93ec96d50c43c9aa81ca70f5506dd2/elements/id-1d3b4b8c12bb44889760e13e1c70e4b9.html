CII via CIP Opt-Out ()
The process by which a customer can request the termination of the CII via CIP service, leading to its subsequent deactivation (opt-out). This process is represented in both the B2C CII via CIP Customer Management and Energy Distribution Grid Operations business domains for the sake of simplification.
